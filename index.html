Privacy Policy
Last updated: March 2025
1. About This App
Smart Learning Adventure ("the App") is an educational worksheet app designed for children aged 1–7. It is operated by Anima Varshney.
2. Information We Collect
When a parent or guardian registers, we collect:
  • Child's name and age — to personalise questions to the right grade level
  • Parent's email address — to restore progress if the app is reinstalled
  • Parent's mobile number — for payment verification and support
  • Learning progress — days completed, credits balance, question history
  • Device type — Android, iPhone, or web browser (for support purposes)
We do not collect location, contacts, photos, or any other device data.
3. How We Use Your Information
  • To deliver age-appropriate question sets for your child
  • To save and restore learning progress across devices
  • To process and verify payments made through PhonePe
  • To provide customer support when requested
  • To improve the app's question content and experience
We never sell your personal data to third parties.
4. Data Storage
Your data is stored in two places:
  • On your device — in the browser's local storage for offline access
  • Supabase cloud database — hosted on secure servers, used for progress backup and restoration
Supabase is SOC 2 Type 2 certified and complies with GDPR. Data may be stored on servers in Singapore or the United States.
5. Children's Privacy
This app is intended for use by children under the supervision of a parent or guardian. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. All registration is performed by the parent or guardian.
In compliance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 (India), parents may request access to, correction of, or deletion of their child's data at any time.
6. Payments
Payments are processed through PhonePe. We do not store your UPI ID, card number, or any payment credentials. Payment verification is handled server-side and only the transaction result (success/failure) is stored.
7. Your Rights
  • Access — request a copy of your data
  • Correction — update incorrect information
  • Deletion — request complete deletion of your account and data
  • Portability — receive your data in a readable format
To exercise these rights, contact us at the details below. We will respond within 30 days.
8. Cookies & Tracking
The app does not use advertising cookies or third-party trackers. We use browser localStorage and sessionStorage solely to maintain your session and save progress.
9. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy occasionally. The updated date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
